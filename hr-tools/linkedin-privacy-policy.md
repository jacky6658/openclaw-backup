# Step1ne LinkedIn Integration Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 16, 2026  
**Last Updated:** February 16, 2026

## Overview
Step1ne ("we", "our", or "us") operates a recruitment automation system that integrates with LinkedIn to help job seekers find career opportunities and employers find qualified candidates.

## Information We Collect via LinkedIn API

When you authorize our application through LinkedIn OAuth 2.0, we may access:

### Member Data Portability API
- Full name
- Profile photo
- Current job title and company
- Work experience history
- Education background
- Skills and endorsements
- Certifications and licenses
- Contact information (email, if publicly available)

### Pages Data Portability API
- Company page posts and analytics
- Follower engagement metrics
- Post performance data

## How We Use Your Information

### For Job Seekers
- Match your profile with suitable job openings
- Import your professional background into our candidate database
- Provide personalized job recommendations
- Reduce manual data entry during application process

### For Employers
- Post job openings to LinkedIn company pages
- Track job post performance and engagement
- Analyze recruitment campaign effectiveness

## Data Storage and Security

- **Authorization Required:** We only access your LinkedIn data after you explicitly grant permission via OAuth 2.0
- **Encryption:** All data transmitted between our system and LinkedIn is encrypted using TLS 1.2+
- **Data Retention:** 
  - Candidate profiles: Retained for 12 months or until you request deletion
  - Analytics data: Retained for 24 months for performance analysis
- **No Permanent Storage Without Consent:** We do not store your LinkedIn credentials

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Revoke Access:** Disconnect our application from your LinkedIn account at any time via LinkedIn Settings > Apps
- **Request Data Deletion:** Contact us at privacy@step1ne.com to request removal of your data
- **Access Your Data:** Request a copy of the data we've collected from your LinkedIn profile
- **Update Information:** Corrections will be reflected automatically when you update your LinkedIn profile

## Data Sharing

We **DO NOT**:
- Sell your LinkedIn data to third parties
- Share your profile with employers without your explicit consent
- Use your data for purposes outside recruitment services

We **MAY** share data with:
- Employers you have explicitly applied to
- Service providers who help us operate our platform (under strict confidentiality agreements)
- Legal authorities if required by law

## LinkedIn API Compliance

This application complies with:
- LinkedIn API Terms of Use
- LinkedIn Developer Program Policy
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- Personal Data Protection Act (Taiwan)

## Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
